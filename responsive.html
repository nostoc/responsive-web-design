The City

Istanbul is the biggest city in Turkey. It connects Asia and Europe with its bridges. The city is culturally rich and a home for generations of many families over centuries.

What?

Istanbul is a city in Turkey and connects Asia and Europe continents.

Where?

It is in the northwest of Turkey and about 300km away from the city of Burgas.

How?

You can reach Istanbul airport from all over the world.
